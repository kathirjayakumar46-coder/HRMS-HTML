HR POLICY: LEAVE MANAGEMENT
Employees can apply for casual leave (CL), sick leave (SL), and earned leave (EL).
Casual Leave: Maximum 12 days per year. Requires at least 1 day prior notice.
Sick Leave: Maximum 15 days per year. Medical certificate required for absences exceeding 3 days.
Earned Leave: Accrues at 1.5 days per month of service. Can be carried forward up to 30 days.
Leave application must include: employee ID, employee name, start date, end date, reason.
Maternity Leave: 26 weeks paid maternity leave as per statutory requirement.
Paternity Leave: 5 days paid paternity leave.
Leave Without Pay (LWP): Granted when leave balance is exhausted. Requires HOD approval.
Priority escalation: Urgent medical situations are treated as HIGH priority tickets.

HR POLICY: PAYROLL AND SALARY
Salary is processed on the last working day of each month.
Salary slip queries should include: employee ID, month, and year of concern.
Deductions include: PF (12% of basic), ESI (if applicable), TDS (as per tax slab).
Advance salary: Up to 2 months salary can be drawn as advance with HOD approval.
Reimbursements: Travel, medical, and meal reimbursements require receipts and manager approval.
Incorrect salary: Must be reported within 7 days of credit. Priority: HIGH.
Arrears: Calculated and processed within 30 days of promotion or pay revision.
Pay revision disputes are URGENT priority if employee has filed formal complaint.

HR POLICY: GRIEVANCE HANDLING
Grievances should be filed within 30 days of the incident.
Types of grievances: Workplace harassment, discrimination, unfair treatment, policy violations.
Grievance ticket priority: HIGH by default, URGENT if safety is at risk.
Resolution timeline: Acknowledgement within 2 working days, resolution within 15 working days.
Anonymous grievances are accepted through the HR portal.
Escalation: If not resolved in 15 days, automatically escalates to HR Head.

HR POLICY: IT SUPPORT
IT support tickets cover: hardware issues, software access, VPN problems, email issues.
Priority levels:
  - URGENT: System down, cannot work at all
  - HIGH: Major functionality broken, workaround exists
  - MEDIUM: Minor issue, work continues with inconvenience
  - LOW: Enhancement request, cosmetic issues
SLA response times: URGENT=2hrs, HIGH=4hrs, MEDIUM=8hrs, LOW=24hrs.
Software access requests require manager approval before provisioning.
Password resets are handled by IT helpdesk within 1 hour.

HR POLICY: TICKET CLASSIFICATION
Leave-related keywords: leave, vacation, sick, medical, absence, off, holiday, fever, emergency
Payroll-related keywords: salary, pay, payslip, reimbursement, advance, deduction, tax, PF
Grievance-related keywords: complaint, harassment, discrimination, unfair, issue with manager
IT Support keywords: laptop, system, software, VPN, email, access, password, network, printer
Priority rules:
  - Medical emergencies: HIGH
  - Safety concerns: URGENT
  - Routine leave: LOW to MEDIUM depending on duration
  - Payroll discrepancies: HIGH
  - IT system down: URGENT
